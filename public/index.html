EVM Security Features

  • Standalone Machine: No wireless, internet, or Bluetooth connectivity.
  • Tamper-Proof Seals: Sealed with signatures of polling agents.
  • Randomisation: EVMs are randomly allocated twice before elections.
  • Mock Polls: Conducted on election morning in presence of candidate agents to verify functionality.

Read more about Voter Rights or check the Myth Busters section.

Did You Know? India uses ~5.5 million EVMs, manufactured exclusively by two public sector undertakings: Bharat Electronics Limited (BEL) and Electronics Corporation of India Limited (ECIL).
